Brand Relevance & Repositioning
For: Listed Co. · Established brands · MNCs facing market shift
Search terms
brand repositioning Singapore · brand relevance strategy Singapore · business strategy consultant Singapore · brand transformation consultant · strategic marketing consultancy Singapore

The most dangerous moment for a brand is not a crisis — it is the slow drift of irrelevance. When your audience's expectations evolve faster than your brand does, the gap quietly widens until a competitor fills it. PI:X helps brands identify that drift early and design the strategic moves that restore momentum.

This work sits at the intersection of business strategy and brand strategy — examining how the brand creates value, how it communicates that value, and whether both remain compelling to the audiences that matter most. As PMC-certified practitioners, we bring the rigour of a strategic review with the creative ambition to see genuine new possibilities, not just incremental improvements.

The PI:X Collective

Strategy is only as good
as the people who execute it.

PI:X is backed by a trusted collective of specialist partners — senior-led firms across every discipline a brand might need, all vetted, all briefed and governed by PI:X. When you brief us, you access the full depth of this Collective under one accountable relationship.

Why the Collective exists

The PI:X Collective was not assembled by a procurement team. It was built through years of professional relationships — practitioners who Celestine and the Piquant Ideas team have worked alongside, referred to clients, and come to trust through real-world delivery.

Many of our Collective partners share a common origin story: senior careers at international agencies or corporations, followed by the decision to build something better — their own firms, their own standards, their own relationships. When PI:X brings them into an engagement, they are not subcontractors. They are co-owners of the outcome.

This matters because the quality of execution is what separates a strategy that works from one that looks good on a slide. PI:X has spent a decade building the network that removes that risk.

How the Collective is governed

Every Collective partner is briefed by PI:X — not by the client. The strategic context, the brand standards, the campaign objectives and the KPIs are held in a single place and communicated consistently across every execution partner involved in the engagement.

The client has one point of contact throughout. One relationship to manage. One team accountable for the outcome. What changes behind that relationship is the depth of specialist capability activated to deliver it — without the overhead, the briefing gaps, or the coordination losses that come from managing multiple vendors directly.

The result: agency-quality execution at boutique-consultancy speed and accountability. It is one of the most difficult things in our industry to build, and it is one of the things that makes PI:X genuinely rare.

About PI:X

We built PI:X because
brands deserve better than a junior team.

The origin of PI:X

PI:X is the go-to-market execution arm of Piquant Ideas Pte. Ltd. — a boutique brand and business consultancy founded in Singapore in 2013. For twelve years, Piquant Ideas built a reputation for insight-led brand strategy that delivers real commercial outcomes: market expansions, brand repositioning, talent attraction and business transformation across eight industries and six countries.

PI:X extends that foundation. It exists because the brands that needed the most strategic help — listed companies, government bodies, MNCs with ambitious growth agendas — were being served by international agencies that sent senior partners to win the pitch, then handed the work to whoever was available. We decided to build the alternative.

At PI:X, the person you meet is the person who does the work. The strategic thinking, the client relationship and the quality accountability all stay in the same hands — backed by a trusted collective of specialist partners that extends our reach without sacrificing our standards.

What makes us genuinely different

We are PMC-certified consultants recognised by Enterprise Singapore and listed on the GoBusiness IPGrow platform — the government's curated directory of verified service providers. This means clients can access government grants for up to 50% of our consultancy fees.

And we are connected to the BNI Singapore network and a curated regional partner ecosystem — which means we can move from strategy to execution in any of six markets without managing that complexity back to the client. One brief. One team. Genuine end-to-end accountability.

Grants & Funding

Senior GTM strategy.
Significantly subsidised.

As a PMC-certified consultancy listed on Enterprise Singapore's GoBusiness IPGrow platform, PI:X helps qualifying Singapore businesses access grants covering up to 50% of go-to-market strategy, brand development and market expansion consultancy costs.

EDG
Enterprise Development Grant
For go-to-market strategy, brand development and business strategy projects
50%funding support

The Enterprise Development Grant is administered by Enterprise Singapore and supports Singapore companies in building capabilities and growing. For PI:X clients, the EDG covers go-to-market strategy, strategic brand development and business strategy development — meaning up to 50% of our consultancy fees can be funded by Enterprise Singapore.

Because PI:X is listed on GoBusiness IPGrow as a verified service provider, your application is backed by a consultancy with a published track record and verified PMC credentials — which significantly improves approval confidence.

You Likely Qualify If:

  • Your company is registered and operating in Singapore
  • You have at least 30% local shareholding
  • Your project falls under GTM Strategy, Brand Development or Business Strategy scope
  • You are engaging a PMC-certified consultant (PI:X is certified)
  • Your project has a defined scope, timeline and measurable outcomes
MRA
Market Readiness Assistance Grant
For Singapore companies expanding into new overseas markets
50%funding support

The Market Readiness Assistance (MRA) Grant helps Singapore businesses take their first steps into overseas markets. PI:X's Market Expansion service is specifically designed to be MRA grant-eligible — covering market entry strategy, brand localisation, market promotion and business development in a new country.

We have helped brands expand into Malaysia, Indonesia, Philippines, Taiwan and Vietnam. The MRA grant makes that expertise accessible at a significantly reduced cost for qualifying Singapore businesses.

You Likely Qualify If:

  • Your company is Singapore-registered with at least 30% local shareholding
  • Annual revenue does not exceed S$100 million, or ≤ 200 employees
  • You are entering a new overseas market (not one you already operate in)
  • Activities include market entry strategy, market promotion or business development

Why our GoBusiness listing matters for your application

PI:X is listed on Enterprise Singapore's GoBusiness IPGrow platform — the government's curated directory of verified service providers. This means your grant application is backed by a consultancy with published credentials, verified delivery track record, and ongoing accountability to a national standard. It materially improves approval confidence and gives your board a clear basis for the engagement decision.
